FAQ

What is Fluenzo?

Fluenzo is an international online and offline community for achieving language fluency with your perfect people! Our innovative formula of forming strong social chemistry and fluency-oriented language teaching fosters hundreds of people around the globe to meet their perfect people and grow fluent in foreign languages together on Fluenzo!

How will Fluenzo help me?

Fluenzo is a mobile app space created for language learners from all over the world to get connected with their Soulmates to practice languages with. On Fluenzo, language practice happens in between the digital and real worlds, with the help of RWW technology. Also, Fluenzo is powered by an AI-algorithm that connects users to their Soulmates after just taking a quick compatibility test in the beginning of the journey.
